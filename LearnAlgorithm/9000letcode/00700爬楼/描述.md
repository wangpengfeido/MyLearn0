难度：简单

你正在爬一个楼梯，需要n阶到顶。每次可以爬1或2阶，有多少种不同的方式登顶？

例一
````
Input: 2
Output: 2
Explanation: There are two ways to climb to the top.
1. 1 step + 1 step
2. 2 steps
````

例二
````
Input: 3
Output: 3
Explanation: There are three ways to climb to the top.
1. 1 step + 1 step + 1 step
2. 1 step + 2 steps
3. 2 steps + 1 step
````
